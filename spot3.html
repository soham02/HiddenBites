Filet Mignon and Brussels

About the Dish

The filet mignon is served with a rich demi-glaze sauce, accompanied by creamy smashed potatoes, roasted broccoli, and crispy onion straws. The dish perfectly balances savory flavors and textures, offering a satisfying and hearty meal.

Why Soham Loves It

I loved this dish for its perfectly charred broccoli, which added a delightful flavor, and the unique garlic butter that complemented the filet mignon beautifully. The combination of textures and flavors made it a memorable meal for me.
