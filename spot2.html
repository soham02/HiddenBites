Sweet Chili Brussels & The IT5 Burger

About the Dish

The IT5 Burger: A rich, flavorful burger layered with creamy Boursin, smoked gouda, and savory roasted garlic onion jam.

Sweet Chili Brussels: Crispy brussels sprouts tossed in sweet chili sauce with bacon bits and topped with crunchy onion straws.

Why Soham Loves It

I love these brussels sprouts for their perfect golden crispiness that adds a satisfying crunch to every bite. The flavorful chili sauce brings a spicy kick that beautifully complements the natural earthiness of the sprouts, making them a standout side dish that’s hard to resist
