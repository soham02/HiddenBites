About the Dish

These hearty bowls of ramen feature a rich, flavorful broth that’s packed with umami goodness. Topped with tender noodles, fresh green onions, and bean sprouts, each bowl is crowned with crispy fried onions, spicy chili threads, and a generous helping of savory pork. The vibrant toppings add both texture and depth, making each spoonful a satisfying blend of flavors and aromas. Perfect for warming up on a chilly day, these ramen bowls are a treat for any ramen lover.
